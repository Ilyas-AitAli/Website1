About Us

Founded in 2019, SmartStart was initially created to help students continue their studies during the COVID-19 pandemic. Since then, we have expanded into a dedicated team of over 70 incredible tutors and teachers. We have successfully guided countless students through a wide range of elementary, high school, and university-level courses. Our mission is to make learning a positive and enriching experience for individuals of all ages. With our expertise and experience, we are committed to helping you reach your full potential.

Why Choose Us

At SmartStart, we understand that being a great tutor requires more than just subject matter expertise. It's equally important to connect with students and make learning enjoyable to ensure successful tutoring sessions. Our tutors at SmartStart not only have extensive experience in their specialized subjects but also possess the skills and expertise to teach the material in an engaging and comprehensible way.
